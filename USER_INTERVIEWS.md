# User Interviews

### Interview 1: The Heavy Researcher (High Spend)
- Name : Rahul Yadav (Final year Tech Student).
- Role : Learning Full-Stack development and doing research.
- Tools Used : ChatGPT Plus($20) + Claude Pro($20) + preplexity Pro($20).
- Monthly spend: $60(~₹5,000).
- Problem (Pian Point): Rahul feels that he is paying for three different tools even though they have similar functionalities.
                        He wants to bring his monthly expenses down to $20.
- Opportunity for Audit Engine: Recommend combining tools or switching to an aggregator like Poe.com or using API-based tools.
