# Project Economics - AI Spend Auditor

### 1. The Problem
Developers often lose track of multiple AI API subscriptions (OpenAI, Anthropic, Midjourney, etc.), leading to "subscription drift" where costs exceed $100/month without notice.

### 2. The Solution (Value Prop)
This tool provides a centralized dashboard to visualize monthly burn. By setting a hard limit (e.g., $60), users can identify high-cost services and switch to cheaper alternatives (like OpenRouter or local LLMs) before the billing cycle ends.

### 3. Cost-Benefit Analysis
- **App Cost:** $0 (Deployed on Vercel Free Tier).
- **User Savings:** Estimated 15-20% reduction in monthly AI spend through better visibility and "Red Alert" budget tracking.
