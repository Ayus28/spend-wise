# Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy - AI Spend Auditor

### 1. Target Audience
- Individual developers using multiple AI APIs (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.).
- Small startup teams tracking burn rates on AI subscriptions.

### 2. Distribution Channels
- **Product Hunt:** Launching as a free tool for the developer community.
- **Twitter/X:** Sharing "Build in Public" updates and budget-saving tips.
- **Developer Forums:** Posting on Reddit (r/webdev) and Dev.to.

### 3. Value Proposition
"Stop guessing your AI bill. Get real-time alerts before you overspend."
